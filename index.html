About 3G Charitable Foundation

A nonprofit organization dedicated to fostering spiritual growth and community support

Established in October 2016 in New York State, USA, the 3G Charitable Foundation has been serving as a beacon of hope and support for religious education initiatives for over 8 years. As a community-focused organization, we are committed to promoting religious education and supporting community initiatives through strategic funding and donations.

Our Mission & Vision

We exist to support religious education, creating opportunities for younger generations to access spiritual knowledge, develop character, and live responsibly within their communities.

We believe that religious education is the foundation for shaping moral citizens who show love and compassion for others. Through our work, we aim to build stronger, more connected communities rooted in spiritual values.
